Electronics Engineering Council


Vision

The USC Electronics Engineering Council is the physical manifestation of the
bridge between the ECE students and the bureaucratic educational system of the
University of San Carlos, and its duty is to express the ideals and promote the welfare
of the ECE student body in order to instill the spirit of unity.

Mission

The USC Electronics Engineering Council, a co-curricular organization under the
Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, is a student based organization
that dedicates itself to protect the rights of ECE students and to hold all request interest.
We aim to promote the Carolinian Moral Character and the advancement of the
students through the encouragement of research, group discussion and other useful
media. This knowledge gained is disseminated amongst the members of the council
to maintain a higher proficiency and effectiveness.
Our responsibility is to maintain activeness and interaction with the ECE student
body. Without student and council interaction, the council cannot exist.

Preamble

We, the Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering students of the
University of San Carlos, invoking the blessings of the Divine Providence.
We are motivated by an intense desire to express our ideals, promote our welfare and protect
our rights. We aim to instill in our selves the spirit of unity and cooperation in order to
enhance moral, intellectual, social and spiritual growth. Feeling the need for a
student organization which will dedicate to the task of applying to human needs the
principles of electronics, communications, and other related fields. Thus, we do bind
