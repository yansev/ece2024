ECE Acquaintance Party


This activity is intended for all the students of USC Electronics Engineering. This activity helps every participant to be acquainted with their peers in the USC ECE Community through different activities in the event. The goal of this activity is to develop a sense of belongingness and to acquaint the new peers to the upperclassmen, getting to know one another through direct interpersonal interactions by the activities and to be able to foster an atmosphere where strong bonds are formed through harmony and cooperation.


Team Building

This team building is intended for the officers of the different organizations under the BS ECE program The goal of this team building is to help student leaders improve their capacity to communicate, collaborate, and get to know each other in the team. Through this, the students would be acquainted and establish connections with one another.
